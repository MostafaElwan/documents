# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 8, 2026

Perspecta ("Perspecta", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how it is shared, and the choices available to you when using the Perspecta mobile application and related services.

By using Perspecta, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Account Information

We may collect:

* Email address (if you create an account or contact support)
* User identifier associated with your account
* Subscription and purchase information

### B. Conversation and Content Data

When you use Perspecta, we may process:

* Topics submitted for discussion
* Messages and prompts you provide
* Responses generated by AI participants
* Uploaded documents or files used as discussion context
* Conversation history and transcripts
* Custom AI friend configurations you create

This information is used solely to provide and improve the service.

### C. Voice and Audio Data

If you use voice features, we may process:

* Audio recordings necessary to convert speech to text
* Voice interaction metadata
* Language preferences

Audio data is processed only to provide voice-related functionality and is not used for advertising purposes.

### D. Usage Information

We may collect information about how you interact with the application, including:

* Features used
* Discussion activity
* Session duration
* Performance metrics
* Crash reports
* Diagnostic information

### E. Device Information

We may collect:

* Device model
* Operating system version
* Application version
* Language settings
* Anonymous device identifiers
* Network and connectivity information

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use information to:

* Provide and maintain Perspecta services
* Generate AI-powered discussions and responses
* Enable voice and multilingual features
* Manage subscriptions and purchases
* Improve application performance and reliability
* Detect abuse, fraud, or unauthorized use
* Respond to support requests
* Comply with legal obligations

## 3. Subscription and Payment Processing

Perspecta offers optional premium subscriptions.

Subscription purchases are processed by the applicable app store, including:

* Apple App Store
* Google Play Store

We do not collect or store your payment card information.

Subscription status and purchase information may be processed through trusted subscription management providers, including RevenueCat.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party providers to support the operation of the application, including:

* RevenueCat for subscription management
* Apple App Store and Google Play for purchases
* Firebase for analytics, diagnostics, and crash reporting
* Cloud infrastructure providers for application hosting and processing
* AI service providers necessary to generate discussions and responses

These providers may process information on our behalf and are subject to their own privacy policies and security practices.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

We may share information only:

* With service providers that help operate the application
* To comply with legal requirements
* To protect our rights, users, or platform security
* In connection with a merger, acquisition, or business transfer

## 6. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to:

* Provide services to users
* Maintain account functionality
* Comply with legal obligations
* Resolve disputes
* Enforce agreements

When information is no longer required, we take reasonable steps to delete or anonymize it.

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

* Access your personal information
* Request correction of inaccurate information
* Request deletion of your information
* Object to certain processing activities
* Request a copy of your data where applicable

Requests may be submitted using the contact information below.

## 8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Perspecta is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided personal information may contact us.

## 10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your country of residence. By using Perspecta, you acknowledge that information may be transferred to and processed in jurisdictions with different data protection laws.

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

When material changes are made, we will update the Effective Date and may provide additional notice through the application or other appropriate means.
