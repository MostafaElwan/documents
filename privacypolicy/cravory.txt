At Cravory, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App")—designed to enhance your food discovery experience.

By using Cravory, you agree to the practices outlined in this Privacy Policy.

🔐 Information We Collect
We only collect information necessary to provide and improve your experience with the app. Currently, we request only two permissions:

1. Location Access (Optional)
We may request access to your device’s current location to provide a personalized and interactive map experience that showcases meals from different parts of the world—including nearby or region-based recommendations.

Your exact location is not stored or shared.
You can deny this permission and still use most of Cravory’s features.
2. Notifications Permission
With your consent, we send push notifications to keep you informed about new meals, weekly plans, personalized suggestions, or app updates.

You can control or disable notifications in your device settings at any time.
🛡️ What Data We Do Not Collect
We do not collect, store, or share any of the following:

Personal identity data (like your name, email, or contact info)
Health or medical records
Payment or financial data
Your precise real-time location (it is used only locally for map display if granted)
🔄 Data Sharing and Third Parties
Cravory does not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties.
Since we do not store personally identifiable information, there is nothing to share or sell. In the future, if any third-party integrations are introduced (e.g. analytics, authentication, etc.), this policy will be updated accordingly—with transparency.

🔧 Security
We are committed to ensuring your information (if any) is secure. Cravory uses appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to protect against unauthorized access or misuse of app functionalities.

👤 Children’s Privacy
Cravory is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal data from children.

⚙️ Your Rights & Controls
You are always in control. You may:

Deny or revoke location permission at any time from your device.
Control or disable notifications from your system settings.
Use most of the app’s features even with permissions turned off.
